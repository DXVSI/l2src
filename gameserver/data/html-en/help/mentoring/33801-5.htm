If you have the right to be a mentor, you can find a character suitable to become a disciple, and enter the name, press the [+] in the Contact / Mentoring. If the character agrees, he will become your disciple.
If you have reached level 85 and is not regenerated, but you have a Certificate of trust, you can become an apprentice.
If you are a pupil or a teacher, but you do not like your partner, you can end the relationship. To do this, click on the [-] to select a character name in Contacts / Mentoring.
